§ 簡介 / Introduction

關於本政策

感謝您使用「台日地震監測」（以下簡稱「本 App」）。本政策說明本 App 在您使用過程中如何收集、使用、儲存與保護您的資料。

本 App 為地震資訊推播服務，主要功能為提供台灣與日本地區的地震速報、震度資訊與推播通知。我們重視您的隱私權，並致力於以最低限度收集必要資訊以提供服務。

重點摘要：本 App 不要求您建立帳號、不收集您的姓名、電話或電子郵件，僅在功能必要範圍內處理少量技術性資料（如裝置推播 Token、廣告識別碼）。

  • 通知門檻：您設定的最低震度通知條件（例如「3 級以上才通知」）。

§ 02

資訊用途

我們僅在以下目的範圍內使用收集的資料：

  • 提供地震推播：根據您的地區與門檻設定，傳送相關地震速報。
  • 顯示廣告：透過 Google AdMob 顯示廣告以維持本 App 免費營運。
  • 改善服務：分析整體使用統計（匿名化）以改善功能。
  • 故障診斷：當推播或功能異常時，技術日誌協助我們修復問題。

我們不會將您的資料出售予第三方、不會用於與服務無關的行銷，亦不會與其他資料庫交叉比對識別您的身份。

為提供完整服務，本 App 使用以下第三方服務，各服務均有其獨立的隱私權政策：

  • P2P 地震情報：日本即時地震速報
  • USGS：美國地質調查局全球地震資料
§ 04

資料儲存與保留

  • 推播 Token：儲存於 Cloudflare KV 資料庫，於您解除安裝 App 或關閉通知後，於 30 天內自動清除。
  • App 內設定：儲存於您裝置本機，不離開您的裝置；解除安裝即清除。
  • 廣告快取：由 Google AdMob 管理，遵循其資料保留規範。

§ Introduction

About This Policy

Thank you for using Taiwan-Japan Earthquake Monitor (hereinafter "the App"). This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the App.

The App is an earthquake notification service providing real-time alerts and intensity information for Taiwan and Japan. We respect your privacy and are committed to collecting only the minimum data necessary to deliver our service.

Summary: The App does not require account registration and does not collect your name, phone number, or email. We only process technical identifiers (push tokens, advertising IDs) strictly necessary for the service.

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Notification Region: Your selected region for earthquake alerts (e.g., "Taipei", "Tokyo"), stored on your device and our notification server.
  • Notification Threshold: Your minimum intensity setting (e.g., "Notify at intensity 3 or above").
  • In-App Preferences: Such as language and dark mode, stored locally only.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, phone number, or email address
  • Precise GPS location (this App does not use location services)
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • Browsing history or usage of other applications
§ 02

How We Use Information

We use collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • Earthquake Notifications: Sending relevant alerts based on your region and threshold settings.
  • Advertising: Displaying ads via Google AdMob to support free operation of the App.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing aggregate usage statistics (anonymized) to improve functionality.
  • Diagnostics: Technical logs to troubleshoot push or feature issues.

We do not sell your data to third parties, use it for unrelated marketing, or cross-reference it with other databases to identify you.

§ 03

Third-Party Services

To deliver the full service, the App integrates the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

3.1 Notifications & Infrastructure

  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): Delivering push notifications. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Workers: Our notification server and configuration service. Cloudflare Privacy Policy

3.2 Advertising

  • Google AdMob: Displays banner ads. AdMob may use data such as IDFA for ad targeting depending on your ATT consent. Google Privacy Policy

3.3 Earthquake Data Sources (Public, no personal data)

  • Central Weather Administration (CWA): Taiwan earthquake data
  • Japan Meteorological Agency (JMA): Japan earthquake data
  • P2P Earthquake Information: Real-time Japanese seismic alerts
  • USGS: United States Geological Survey global earthquake data
§ 04

Data Storage & Retention

  • Push Tokens: Stored in Cloudflare KV; automatically purged within 30 days after uninstallation or notification opt-out.
  • In-App Settings: Stored locally on your device; cleared upon uninstallation.
  • Advertising Cache: Managed by Google AdMob according to their retention policies.
§ 05

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences and contains no content specifically targeting children. If a guardian has concerns, please contact us via the email below.

§ 06

  • Remove All Data: Uninstall the App to clear all locally stored settings and caches.
  • Access & Deletion Requests: If you wish to query or delete data we store, please contact us via the email below; we will respond within 30 days.
